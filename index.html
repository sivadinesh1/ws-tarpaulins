About Us

For over 20 years, Rohini Tarpaulins is one of the leading tarpaulin manufacturer of South India. Rohini Tarpaulin products have earned accolades for their high quality and trustworthy durability.


Rohini Tarpaulins manufacture all types of Cotton canvas, HDPE and Synthetic Tarpaulins that are used in Trucks, Haulers, Cargo Fleets, Industrial Shelters, Food storage and various other generic applications. The product portfolio covers 5 categories and more than 23 products.


Headquartered in Chennai, Rohini Tarpaulins has established a strong sales and support network across South India, as well as International Exports.
